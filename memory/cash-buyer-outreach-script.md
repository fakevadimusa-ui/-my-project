# Cash Buyer Outreach Script

---

## Phone Call Script (cold call to known buyers like Ozarks House Buyers)

> "Hey, my name is Vadim — I'm a wholesaler here in Springfield. I'm building my cash buyer list and wanted to reach out because you guys are active in the area. I'm focusing on zip codes 65802 and 65803 — distressed properties, off-market deals. I wanted to ask — what's your buy box look like? What are you actively looking for right now?"

**Then listen. Take notes. Ask follow-ups:**
- "What's the max price point you're comfortable at?"
- "How fast do you typically close once you see a deal you like?"
- "Best way to send you deals — text, email?"
- "Mind if I add you to my buyer list?"

**Close with:**
> "Perfect. I'll be in touch as soon as I have something that fits. I appreciate your time."

---

## Text/DM Script (for Craigslist, Facebook, cold text)

> "Hey [Name] — I'm Vadim, a wholesaler based in Springfield. I came across your info and wanted to connect. I focus on off-market deals in 65802/65803. Are you actively buying right now? If so, what's your buy box?"

Keep it short. If they reply, transition to the phone call script above.

---

## Facebook Group Post Script

> "Hey everyone — I'm Vadim, a local wholesaler here in Springfield building my cash buyer list. I focus on off-market, discounted properties in Greene County (65802, 65803).
>
> If you're an active cash buyer looking for deals in this area, drop a comment or DM me. I just want to know your buy box — zip codes, property type, price range, and how fast you close.
>
> Let's connect and make some deals happen."

---

## Voicemail Script (if no answer)

> "Hey, this is Vadim — I'm a wholesaler in Springfield. I'm building my cash buyer list and wanted to connect since you're active in the area. Give me a call back when you get a chance — I'd love to learn your buy box. My number is [YOUR NUMBER]. Thanks."

---

## Notes
- Always lead with value: you bring deals, they bring cash — it's a partnership
- Don't pitch a specific deal until you know their buy box
- Follow up 2x if no response before moving on
- Log every contact in your buyer list tracker
